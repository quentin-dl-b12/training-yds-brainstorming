# The risk of not engaging

**Teams without structured AI workflows** will see others:
- Deliver features faster
- Ship with fewer security defects
- Produce better compliance evidence

**Ungoverned AI adoption** (vibe coding, raw prompts, no verification) creates new risks:
- Insecure AI-generated code shipped without review
- Compliance gaps where accountability is unclear
- Confidentiality leakage through cloud LLM submissions

> The sweet spot: **governed, structured, human-reviewed AI use.**
> Which is exactly what this workshop has been about.
