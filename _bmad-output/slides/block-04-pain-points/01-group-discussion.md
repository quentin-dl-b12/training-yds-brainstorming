# Group discussion — where does it hurt?

Split into groups of 3–4. Discuss for **12 minutes**.

**Three questions:**

1. In your current projects, where does enforcing security feel most painful or improvable?

2. If you could automate one security-related task in your workflow, what would it be?

3. Where does security knowledge come from in your team — a go-to person, a defined process, or whoever happens to think about potential issues?

---

*Readout: one sentence per group per question — 8 minutes*
