# What this session is — and is not

**This is a workshop, not a lecture.**

- No exam. No certification. No right answers.
- Bring your skepticism. Use it.
- Goal: leave with **one thing you want to try on Monday**

**What we will not do:**
- Cover every AI tool that exists
- Make you an SSDLC expert in 4 hours
- Tell you AI is perfect or harmless

> "We are not here to tell you to use AI blindly.
> We are here to show you where it fits — and where it doesn't."
